ResearchPigeon

Overview

ResearchPigeon is a personalised daily digest for arXiv papers.

The goal is to help researchers keep up with publications in their field without having to scroll through hundreds of new papers every day.

Signed-up users will receive an email each morning containing three new papers tailored to their interests.

No adverts. No hidden costs. No shoehorned AI features. Just signal.

How does it work?

Create a profile describing your research interests in a single sentence.

Every morning, we scan new arXiv papers, rank them against your profile, and email you the three most relevant.

Papers from your digests are automatically saved in ResearchPigeon, so you can come back and review them anytime.
